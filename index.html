Shuang Qiu I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Systems Engineering and Department of Computer Science (secondary) at City University of Hong Kong since 2025. Prior to CityU, I did research in the Department of Mathematics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology as a research assistant professor working with Prof. Tong Zhang (张潼) and in the Booth School of Business at the University of Chicago as a postdoc researcher working with Prof. Mladen Kolar and Prof. Zhaoran Wang. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Prof. Jieping Ye (叶杰平).
