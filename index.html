Research Interest
My recent research interests generally focus on sequential decision-making problems and their applications in Agentic AI, Large Language Models, and Embodied AI. Specifically, my research interest include both applications and theories of reinforcement learning, game theory, large language models, robotics, AI agents, diffusion models, and nonconvex optimization.
